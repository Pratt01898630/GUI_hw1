Aaron Pratt's Discography

The following website contains the discography of Aaron Pratt. Aaron is a 21-year-old college student who produces electronic music on the side.

Songs

Here are his latest tracks in 2023:

Timeless

Timeless is a progressive house track that was released in September 2023. It features melodic lead synthesizers and repetitive buildups. Timeless is an emotional track, similar to songs produced by Martin Garrix.

Holiday (Remix)

Holiday (Remix) is a pop/dance track that was released in July 2023. The vocals from "Holiday" by KSI, were used to create this version. Holiday (Remix) aims to provide a chill summer vibe.

Don't Let Me Down - Hardstyle Remake

Don't Let Me Down - Hardstyle Remake is an electronic track that was released in February 2023. This song is a remake of the Hardwell and Sephyx version. This track contains vocals, high-pitch saws, lead synthesizers, and vibrating kick drums to simulate the hardstyle feel.

Rise Above (Avicii Tribute)

Rise Above (Avicii Tribute) is an electronic track that was released in February 2023. This song was created to honor Avicii, who was a pioneer and icon for electronic dance music. He passed away five years prior. The song creates an optimistic feel as it uses the authentic style of Avicii.
